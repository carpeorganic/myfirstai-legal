Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-30 · Last updated: 2026-06-02

Carpe (hereinafter the “Company”) values the privacy of its users and complies with the Personal Information Protection Act and other applicable laws. This policy explains how the MyFirstAI app and related services (the “Service”) handle personal information.

1. Personal Information Collected and How It Is Collected

The Company collects and uses the following items.

CategoryItemsMethodPurpose
Device & usage data (required) On-device anonymous identifier (a random ID generated by the app without sign-up), device model & OS version, app version, language/region settings, usage logs (access time, screen navigation, feature usage frequency), diagnostic/error information. Advertising identifiers (IDFA/AAID) are NOT collected (see Section 9). Generated and collected automatically when the app runs (the Company’s own backend) Maintaining service stability, diagnosing errors, analyzing usage statistics, improving features
Advertising data (Android, when ads are shown) Approximate location (estimated from IP), app interactions (app launches, taps, video views), diagnostic information, device/other identifiers (App Set ID, etc. — excluding the advertising ID (AAID)) Collected and transmitted by the ad SDK when ads (AdMob) are shown on Android. No ads are served on iOS, so this data is not collected there. Serving non-personalized (contextual) ads, ad performance measurement, fraud prevention
Payment information (for in-app purchases) Transaction identifier (order number), product name, payment amount, payment date/time, receipt information (issued by Apple App Store / Google Play) Collected through the payment platform when the user makes an in-app purchase Payment verification, delivery of subscriptions/items, refund and dispute handling, accounting and tax processing
AI feature usage information Text and other inputs the user provides to the AI feature, and the AI response results. A child’s nickname and AI-friend name are replaced with a pseudonymous token before transmission; the actual values are never sent to the external AI. Collected and transmitted when the user uses the AI feature Generating AI responses and improving service quality. Not stored on the Company’s servers; transmitted to an external AI service provider, after which the response is received and the data is immediately discarded.

Because the Service is provided without a sign-up process, the Company does not collect directly identifying information such as name, email, or phone number.

2. Retention and Use Period

  • Device & usage data: stored for up to 12 months from the date of collection, then automatically destroyed. De-identified aggregate data for statistical purposes may be retained without a time limit.
  • Payment information: retained for 5 years in accordance with the Act on Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce (records of contracts or withdrawal of subscription).
  • AI feature usage information: discarded immediately after the AI response is generated. Not stored on the Company’s servers.

3. Provision to Third Parties

The Company does not provide users’ personal information to third parties beyond the scope stated in this policy, except in the following cases.

  • When the user has given prior consent
  • When required by law, or when an investigative agency requests it in accordance with the procedures prescribed by law for investigative purposes

4. Outsourcing of Personal Information Processing

For smooth provision of the Service, the Company outsources personal information processing tasks as follows.

5. Transfer Abroad

Some processors (e.g., Apple Inc., Google LLC, external AI service providers) are located overseas or operate overseas servers. In such cases, the Company outsources only to operators with secure transfer and processing procedures in accordance with applicable law. The items, purpose, country, timing, and method of transfer are described in the “4. Outsourcing of Personal Information Processing” table above.

6. Users’ Rights and How to Exercise Them

Users may exercise the following rights at any time.

  • Request to access, correct, delete, or suspend the processing of personal information
  • Data deletion request: In the app, go to Parent area → Safety settings → Data deletion request to send an email (with your user ID auto-attached) requesting deletion of server-side data. In addition, uninstalling the app deletes the data stored on the device (nickname, settings, progress).
  • Request an in-app purchase refund (Apple App Store: reportaproblem.apple.com / Google Play: request from purchase history at play.google.com)

These rights may also be exercised by contacting the email at the end of this policy, and the Company will act without delay. Because there is no sign-up, users are distinguished only by an anonymous identifier (ID); deleting server-side data requires providing that ID.

7. Destruction Procedure and Method

Personal information whose retention period has elapsed or whose processing purpose has been achieved is destroyed without delay.

  • Electronic files: permanently deleted so they cannot be recovered or reproduced
  • Non-electronic forms such as printouts (if any): shredded or incinerated

8. Measures to Ensure Security

  • Administrative measures: establishment and implementation of an internal management plan, regular self-inspection
  • Technical measures: access control, encryption of transmission sections (HTTPS/TLS), intrusion prevention systems
  • Physical measures: access control to servers and data rooms where data is stored

9. Advertising and Advertising Identifiers

This app does not use advertising identifiers (IDFA/AAID). On Android, the advertising-ID permission is blocked and only child-directed, non-personalized (contextual) ads are used, so no behavior-based personalized ads are served. No ads are served on iOS. Ads appear only in the parent area; there are no ads on the screens children use.

10. Protection of Children (Under 13)

This Service is designed primarily for children and complies with the Apple Kids Category and Google Play Designed for Families policies, as well as the U.S. COPPA and applicable Korean laws. For children, the following apply.

  • No personalized (behavior-based) advertising is served, and no advertising identifier is collected.
  • A child’s potentially real-name input (nickname, AI-friend name) is replaced with a pseudonymous token, so actual values are never sent to the external AI.
  • Purchases and external links are accessible only behind a parental gate (math problem + PIN).
  • There is no sign-up, so directly identifying information such as name, email, or phone number is not collected.
  • A legal guardian may at any time request access, correction, deletion, or suspension of processing of the child’s personal information, via the in-app Data deletion request or the contact at the end of this policy.
